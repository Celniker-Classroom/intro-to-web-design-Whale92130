My Involvement in Science Olympiad

I compete on the Science Olympiad(or scioly for short) team for CCA. I am very passionate about scioly because it is my way of contributing to the school and doing something I enjoy. The competition is set up where a team competes over 23 events with 15 people, but most people will do 3-4 events because most events require teams of 2 or 3. You are given rules for your assigned specific event, whether it's about chemistry or building a robot, and then you study/build and prepare for the test/challenge for your event. I mainly choose to participate in the build events, where you build a device and use it instead of taking a test on a subject. I buy materials and build my devices based on the parameters of the event, then most of the remaining time is used for testing because it is very crucial to succeed.
